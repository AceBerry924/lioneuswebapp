Lioneus Store

Lioneus Store helps you get your store and product info into Lioneus and make it available to shoppers across Lioneus.

Get started

Here’s what you can do with your Lioneus Store

Upload accurate product information

Give your customers up to date details. Your store helps you ensure that your product info, like price and availability, shows up correctly to potential customers on Lioneus.

Reach customers across Lioneus

Your Lioneus Store powers shopping experiences beyond listing. Show your products to shoppers across search and discovery channels by opting your info into surfaces across Lioneus, Discovery, Shopping Actions, and other Lioneus services.

Discover

Get your products featured in our discovery page where customers will see different types of products each day!

Even more benefits to help you drive growth

Sell your products globally

Lioneus are now available in North America and Europe, which means your products can go global. Set up multi-country feeds in Lioneus Store to start showing your inventory to potential shoppers all around the world.

Highlight promotions

Shoppers say they use Lioneus to find the best deal. Use your store to set up promotions for your items. Set up a site wide promotion to apply to every product on your site, or restrict the promotion to select products.

Improve results with customized opportunities

Lioneus Store doesn’t just help you organize your product and business data, it helps you improve your results too. Inside the Opportunities page of Lioneus Store, you can get a better sense of ways to improve your product data or campaigns to drive more clicks.
